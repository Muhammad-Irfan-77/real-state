About Luxe Realty

Redefining luxury real estate across North America.

Our Heritage

Founded in 1998, Luxe Realty has been the premier destination for high-end residential real estate. We specialize in marketing and selling the most extraordinary homes, estates, and penthouses in the world.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to provide an unparalleled, highly personalized real estate experience for discerning clients. We believe that buying or selling a luxury home should be as seamless and exceptional as the properties themselves.

Global Reach, Local Expertise

With offices in Toronto, New York, Miami, and Beverly Hills, our network of elite agents possesses deep local market knowledge combined with a global marketing reach. We connect sellers with international buyers and help buyers find hidden gems before they even hit the market.
