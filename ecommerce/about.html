How NOVA Started

Founded in 2019 in Milan, NOVA began as a small collective of designers who believed that premium fashion shouldn't require compromising on ethics or breaking the bank. Our founders, Sarah Delacroix and Marco Ferretti, wanted to create a brand that stood at the intersection of craft, sustainability, and modern elegance.

What started as a 12-piece capsule collection on a rented runway quickly grew into one of the most sought-after lifestyle brands of the decade. Today, NOVA serves over 50,000 customers across 32 countries, with flagship stores in Milan, Dubai, and New York.

Our Philosophy

Every NOVA product is designed with a singular belief: that the clothes you wear should make you feel extraordinary. We source only the finest materials — Italian leather, Egyptian cotton, Swiss-grade hardware — and every stitch is reviewed by our master craftspeople before it reaches you.

We refuse to rush our collections. Every season, we release a curated range of items that are built to last years, not one season. This is slow fashion done right.
